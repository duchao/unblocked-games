Privacy Policy for Play Unblocked Games

Introduction

Welcome to Play Unblocked Games, your destination for fun and safe classroom gaming. At Play Unblocked Games, we prioritize your privacy, especially for our younger users. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our gaming platform.

Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better gaming experience:

  1. Usage Information

    • What We Collect: Information about how you interact with our games, including which games you play and how often.
    • Purpose: To improve our game selection and enhance your gaming experience.

  2. Device Information

    • What We Collect: Basic data about the devices you use to access our games, such as device type, operating system, and browser type.
    • Purpose: To optimize our platform for different devices and ensure compatibility.

  3. Cookies

    • What We Collect: Small data files placed on your device that help us track preferences and improve user experience.
    • Purpose: To enhance the functionality of our platform and remember your preferences.

  4. Game Progress

    • What We Collect: Information about your progress in games when you choose to save it.
    • Purpose: To allow you to continue your games where you left off.

Child Privacy

Our platform is designed to be child-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at support@play-unblocked.games.

Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your information seriously. The data we collect is stored securely, and we implement various security measures to protect against unauthorized access. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
